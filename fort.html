20 Famous Forts of Maharashtra

Explore the historical glory of Maharashtra's hill forts

1. Raigad Fort

Raigad was the capital of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj‚Äôs kingdom. Located in the Sahyadri range, it stands 820 metres above sea level.

2. Shivneri Fort

Birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, located near Junnar in Pune district. The fort has strong walls and seven gates.

3. Pratapgad Fort

Built by Shivaji Maharaj, famous for the battle between Shivaji and Afzal Khan. Located near Mahabaleshwar.

4. Sinhagad Fort

Located near Pune, known for the valor of Tanaji Malusare. Offers panoramic views and a popular spot for trekking.

5. Torna Fort

First fort captured by Shivaji Maharaj at the age of 16. Also called Prachandagad, it is located in Pune district.

6. Lohagad Fort

Located near Lonavala, known for its beautiful architecture and the famous "Scorpion's Tail" (Vinchu Kata).

7. Vijaydurg Fort

A sea fort located in Sindhudurg district, built by Shivaji Maharaj. One of the strongest marine forts in Maharashtra.

8. Daulatabad Fort

Near Aurangabad, originally known as Devagiri, this fort is famous for its complex defense systems and strategic location.

9. Harihar Fort

Located in Nashik, the fort is popular for its steep rock-cut staircase and is a thrilling trek destination.

10. Harishchandragad Fort

Ancient fort in Ahmednagar district, known for its scenic beauty, Kedareshwar cave temple, and the Konkan Kada cliff.

11. Kaldurg Fort

Located near Palghar, Kaldurg Fort offers a twin-peak hilltop with panoramic views of the Arabian Sea and the surrounding region.

12. Murud Janjira Fort

A unique sea fort located on an island off the coast of Murud in the Raigad district, famous for being unconquered.

13. Shaniwarwada

Located in Pune, this palace fort was the seat of the Peshwas. It is known for its historical significance and architecture.

14. Sindhudurg Fort

Located on an island in the Arabian Sea, built by Shivaji Maharaj to counter rising sea threats. Known for its hidden entrance.

15. Panhala Fort

Located near Kolhapur, it played an important role in Maratha history and offers panoramic views of the valley.

16. Sajjangad Fort

Resting place of Sant Ramdas, the spiritual guru of Shivaji Maharaj. A revered place located near Satara.

17. Korigad Fort

Located near Lonavala, this hill fort is easy to climb and features two large ponds on top and scenic ramparts.

18. Visapur Fort

Neighboring Lohagad, Visapur is a large fort with lush surroundings, making it a favorite among monsoon trekkers.

19. Karnala Fort

Located in Raigad district, inside the Karnala Bird Sanctuary, it offers a short trek and rich biodiversity.

20. Tringalwadi Fort

Situated in Nashik, this fort has a unique hill shape and offers a commanding view of the surrounding Sahyadri hills.
